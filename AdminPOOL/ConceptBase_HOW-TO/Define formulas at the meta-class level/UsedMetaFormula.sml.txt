* Return all meta formulas that have been partiually evaluated;
* such meta formulas are characterised as assertions that have
* other assertions as explicit subclasses.
*
*}


UsedMetaFormula in QueryClass isA  MSFOLassertion with
  constraint cumf: $ exists msfol/MSFOLassertion :(msfol isA this): $
end
