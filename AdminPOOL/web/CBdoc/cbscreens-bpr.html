Screenshots on Business Process Modeling

Note: This page has not yet been updated for CB 7.0!

A graphical excerpt of a business process model. The modeling language is shown in the smaller graph browser and consists of the four concepts Akteur (agent), Traeger (data carrier), Aktion (aktion), and Daten (data). It was specifically designed in a two-day workshop for the purposes of the consulting company. The bigger graph browser shows instances of these four concepts. The example shows how a specific data carrier (Spesenbeleg=travel expense receipt) flows from an employee (Mitarbeiter) thru the organisation to the customer (Kunde) who has to pay for it.


The workbench shows a query for analyzing a business process model. The example is taken from a cooperation with a consulting company (see paper by Nissen et al. for details). The window at the right shows a selection of the 80 queries that were developed during the cooperation to analyze the business process model. The query shown in the text window classifies those business process models with a certain weakness in the data processing.

Screenshots and models created in cooperation with USU and Hans Nissen.
